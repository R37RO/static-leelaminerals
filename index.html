About Us :

Established in 2020, LEELA MINERALS has become one of the leading manufacturer, supplier of SOAP STONE LUMPS/POWDER (TALC POWDER).Our manufacturing unit is located in Haldwani (Uttarakhand) which is efficiently equipped with modern machineries
The company has gained good clients in a lesser time due to our effective working style and good service. We successfully cater to the needs of our clients with quality product at economical prices. Empowered with well-defined infrastructure, we are very well experienced in handling bulk orders. We are dedicated to achieve total customer satisfaction through the quality product, services and on time deliveries.
